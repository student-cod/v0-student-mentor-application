# Student Mentor Application (LearnAxis)

A modern web application developed to streamline student information access, enhance mentor–mentee collaboration, and support academic and career growth through a unified digital platform.

Live Application: https://v0-student-mentor-application-two.vercel.app/

---

## Hackathon Context

This prototype was built during **Omnitrics – 2025**, an **8-hour national-level hackathon** conducted as part of a tech symposium at **National College, Bengaluru**. The project was collaboratively developed by a **three-member team**, focusing on solving real-world challenges faced in academic mentorship systems within a limited time frame.

---

## Overview

The Student Mentor Application (LearnAxis) addresses common challenges in educational institutions where students face difficulties in accessing academic information and maintaining consistent communication with mentors. The platform provides a structured environment that enables transparent information flow, effective mentorship, and career-oriented guidance.

The application features separate dashboards for students and mentors, allowing both roles to interact efficiently while maintaining centralized access to academic data. An important highlight of the platform is the inclusion of internship opportunities, helping students transition smoothly from academics to professional exposure.

---

## Core Features

### Student Dashboard
Provides students with easy access to essential academic details, including:
- Attendance records  
- Fee information  
- Assignment updates  
- Centralized view of all student-related data  
- Direct interaction with assigned mentors  

---

### Mentor Dashboard
Allows mentors to:
- Manage and monitor assigned students  
- Track academic progress efficiently  
- Provide structured guidance for academic and career decisions  
- Improve mentorship effectiveness through organized interaction  

---

### Seamless Mentor–Student Interaction
- Designed to minimize communication gaps  
- Enables clarity, consistent guidance, and continuous support  
- Strengthens mentor–mentee relationships through structured engagement  

---

### Internship Opportunities (Unique Feature)
- Offers access to internship opportunities directly within the platform  
- Bridges the gap between academics and real-world experience  
- Encourages career-oriented learning alongside formal education  

---

### User-Friendly & Accessible Design
- Clean and intuitive user interface  
- Responsive design for seamless access across devices  
- Focused on usability for both students and mentors  

---

## Purpose & Impact

The Student Mentor Application aims to:
- Centralize academic information for students  
- Strengthen mentor–mentee engagement  
- Improve transparency and accessibility  
- Support students in both academic development and career readiness  

By combining mentorship, academic tracking, and internship access, the platform provides a holistic solution tailored to modern educational environments.
